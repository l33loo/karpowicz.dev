About me

Hello! I'm Lila Karpowicz, a junior front-end web developer.

In a past life, I worked as a medical ethics professional, but fell in love with programming when I picked it up as a hobby a couple of years ago.

I have since completed a web development bootcamp, and worked as a tech support rep in a startup and as a web developer in a digital marketing agency. I have worked with React, jQuery, Wordpress, Symfony, and Bootstrap.

What I love about development is getting in the zone, learning, and facing new challenges everyday. I have great attention to detail and a knak for creating user-friendly experiences.

When I'm not coding, I can be seen mushroom foraging, dancing, or reading (currently catching up on Russian classics).
